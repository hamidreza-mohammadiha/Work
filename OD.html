Hierarchical (Pyramid) Structure

A traditional top-down structure with clear chains of command. Authority flows from the top down through multiple management layers. This structure is common in large, established organizations and provides clear reporting relationships and career progression paths.

Best for: Large corporations, government agencies, traditional manufacturing

Key characteristic: Clear authority lines, defined hierarchy, multiple management layers
